<p><p>The air humidifier market features various types, including vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers operate by boiling water to release steam, effectively increasing humidity levels in a room. They are energy-efficient and ideal for larger spaces. In contrast, water spray humidifiers disperse a fine mist of water directly into the air, providing immediate moisture and comfort. Both types serve to improve air quality, alleviate dryness, and enhance overall well-being in indoor environments.</p></p>
