<p><p>The biconical antennas market is divided into two primary types: omni-directional and directional antennas. Omni-directional antennas radiate and receive signals uniformly across all directions, making them ideal for applications requiring broad coverage, such as in wireless communication and broadcasting. In contrast, directional antennas focus their signal in a specific direction, enhancing range and clarity for targeted applications, like point-to-point communication or radar systems. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including telecommunications and military communications.</p></p>
