<p><p>Bioresorbable coronary stents are innovative medical devices designed to provide temporary support to arteries after a balloon angioplasty procedure. Unlike traditional metal stents that remain permanently in the body, these stents are made from materials that gradually dissolve over time, allowing natural vessel healing and restoring physiological function without the long-term complications associated with permanent implants. </p><p>The bioresorbable coronary stents market is witnessing significant growth due to advancements in biomaterials and increased awareness of cardiovascular diseases. The rising prevalence of coronary artery disease, coupled with a growing aging population, is driving demand for effective and less invasive treatment options.
