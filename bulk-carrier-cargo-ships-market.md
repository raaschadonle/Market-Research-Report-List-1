<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bulk Carrier Cargo Ships Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geared bulk carriers</li><li>Combined carriers</li><li>Gearless carriers</li><li>Self-dischargers</li><li>Lakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bulk carrier cargo ships are categorized based on their loading and unloading capabilities. Geared bulk carriers have onboard cranes for loading and unloading cargo without port equipment. Combined carriers can transport both bulk and liquid cargo. Gearless carriers rely on shore equipment for handling cargo, while self-dischargers utilize onboard mechanisms like conveyor belts for efficient unloading. Lakers are specialized vessels designed for navigating inland waterways and are often smaller to accommodate specific regional trade routes, particularly in lakes.</p></p>
