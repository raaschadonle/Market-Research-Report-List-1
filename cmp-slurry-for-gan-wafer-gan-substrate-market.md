<p><p>CMP slurry for GaN wafers is essential in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring smooth surface finishes. There are primarily two types: colloidal silica polishing slurry and alumina polishing slurry. Colloidal silica slurry utilizes fine silica particles suspended in liquid, providing excellent planarization and minimal defects. Conversely, alumina polishing slurry, containing aluminum oxide particles, is effective for removing material at a higher rate. Both types are crucial for achieving the desired quality and performance in GaN substrates used in high-power and high-frequency applications.</p></p>
