<p><p>The cocoa powder market consists of two main types: natural cocoa powder and alkalized (Dutch-process) cocoa powder. Natural cocoa powder is made from roasted cacao beans and has a strong, slightly acidic flavor, making it ideal for recipes that require leavening agents. In contrast, alkalized cocoa powder undergoes an additional process with alkali, resulting in a milder, darker powder that dissolves easily and is often used in baking and chocolate products where a smoother flavor is desired.</p></p>
