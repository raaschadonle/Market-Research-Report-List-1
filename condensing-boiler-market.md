<p><p>The condensing boiler market is categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on the design and configuration of the boiler units. Vertical condensing boilers are typically space-saving, making them ideal for smaller installations where height is less of a constraint. In contrast, horizontal condensing boilers are often more efficient in terms of heat exchange and are preferred for larger systems that require high output. Both designs aim to maximize energy efficiency and reduce emissions while catering to different spatial and operational needs.</p></p>
