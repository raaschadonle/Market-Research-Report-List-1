<p><ul><li>Conveyor Belt Dryers</li><li>Infrared Conveyor Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conveyor dryers market primarily includes two types: conveyor belt dryers and infrared conveyor dryers. Conveyor belt dryers utilize a continuous belt system to transport products through a heated drying chamber, ensuring uniform drying and high capacity. In contrast, infrared conveyor dryers use radiant heat to quickly dry materials, making them more energy-efficient and suitable for heat-sensitive products. Both types cater to various industries, offering tailored solutions for effective drying processes while optimizing production efficiency.</p></p>
