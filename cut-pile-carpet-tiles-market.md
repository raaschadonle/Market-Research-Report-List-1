<p><p>Cut pile carpet tiles are categorized into three main types: plush pile, Saxony pile, and frieze pile. Plush pile features a soft, dense surface, offering a luxurious feel, ideal for formal spaces. Saxony pile, with its slightly twisted fibers, provides a smoother appearance, suitable for both casual and formal settings. Frieze pile has tightly twisted fibers that create a textured look, adding durability and a casual vibe, making it perfect for high-traffic areas. Each type caters to varying aesthetic preferences and functional needs.</p></p>
