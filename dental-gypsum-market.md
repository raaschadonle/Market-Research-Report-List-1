<p><p>The dental gypsum market encompasses three main types: dental plaster, model dental stone, and die dental stone. Dental plaster is primarily used for creating study casts and models due to its ease of manipulation. Model dental stone offers greater strength and accuracy, making it ideal for producing high-quality dental models. Die dental stone is the most durable, ensuring precise replicas for dental restorations and prosthetics, essential for high-stress applications in restorative dentistry. Each type serves unique functions in dental practices.</p></p>
