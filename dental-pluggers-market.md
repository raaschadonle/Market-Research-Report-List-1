<p><p>The dental pluggers market consists of two main types: double-ended and single-ended pluggers. Double-ended pluggers feature two distinct working ends, allowing for versatility in dental procedures by enabling practitioners to switch between different tips without needing to change instruments. Single-ended pluggers, on the other hand, have a single working end, providing precision for specific tasks. Both types are essential for compacting and shaping materials used in endodontics, restorative dentistry, and filling procedures, catering to various clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
