<p><strong>The Diagnostic Electrocardiograph (ECG) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter ECG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diagnostic Electrocardiograph (ECG) market encompasses three primary types: Resting ECG, Stress ECG, and Holter ECG. Resting ECG records the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest, aiding in initial diagnoses. Stress ECG monitors heart function during physical exertion, identifying cardiovascular issues under stress. Holter ECG is a continuous monitoring tool worn for 24 hours or more, capturing transient heart abnormalities during daily activities. Together, these types enhance cardiac health assessment and disease detection.</p></p>
