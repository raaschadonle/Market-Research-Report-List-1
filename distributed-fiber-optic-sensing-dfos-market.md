The key market types include Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS), which measures temperature profiles along the fiber, and Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), which detects vibrations for security and structural health monitoring. Other emerging technologies, such as Distributed Strain Sensing (DSS), also leverage fiber optics for specific applications. DFOS offers real-time data and is increasingly employed in industries such as oil and gas, infrastructure, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
