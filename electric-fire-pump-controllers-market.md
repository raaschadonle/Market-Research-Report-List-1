Dual source controllers, on the other hand, support multiple power sources, offering redundancy and enhanced reliability for larger or more complex fire protection systems. These controllers facilitate seamless transitions between power sources, ensuring that fire pumps operate even during power failures, thereby enhancing safety and protection measures.</p></p>
