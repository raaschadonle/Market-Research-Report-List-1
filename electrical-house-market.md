<p><p>The electrical house market comprises Low Voltage E-Houses and Medium Voltage E-Houses, each serving distinct roles in power distribution. Low Voltage E-Houses are compact, modular systems designed for managing electricity at voltages below 1 kV, ensuring efficient energy distribution in industrial and commercial applications. In contrast, Medium Voltage E-Houses operate between 1 kV and 36 kV, handling higher energy demands for larger infrastructures like mines and renewable energy plants. Both types enhance reliability, safety, and ease of installation in diverse environments.</p></p>
