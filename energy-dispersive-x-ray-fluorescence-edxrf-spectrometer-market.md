<p><p>Energy-Dispersive X-Ray Fluorescence (EDXRF) Spectrometer is an analytical technique used to determine the elemental composition of materials. This method is widely used across various industries, including environmental monitoring, materials science, and geology, due to its non-destructive nature and ability to provide rapid results.</p><p>The EDXRF market is experiencing significant growth, driven by technological advancements, increasing demand in quality control processes, and a rise in applications in diverse fields.
