Thermoelectric systems convert temperature differences into electrical energy. Piezoelectric materials generate power from mechanical stress or vibrations, often used in sensors or wearable devices. Electrodynamic harvesters exploit movement or changing magnetic fields to generate electricity, suitable for applications in remote monitoring and mobility. Each type offers unique advantages and fits specific applications based on energy sources.</p></p>
