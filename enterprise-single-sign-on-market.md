<p><p>Enterprise Single Sign-On (SSO) solutions are categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud. On-premise SSO is deployed within an organization's own data center, offering greater control and customization, but requires significant maintenance and IT resources. In contrast, cloud-based SSO provides a subscription model that allows for faster deployment, scalability, and reduced IT burden, as it is managed by a third-party provider. Both types enhance user experience by streamlining access to multiple applications securely.</p></p>
