<p><strong>The Fixed Wiring Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel Wire Armoured</li><li>Aluminium Wire Armoured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed wiring cables market comprises primarily two types: Steel Wire Armoured (SWA) and Aluminium Wire Armoured (AWA) cables. SWA cables are designed for robust mechanical protection, featuring steel tape or wire armor, making them suitable for underground or outdoor applications. In contrast, AWA cables utilize aluminum for their armoring, providing a lighter alternative with good corrosion resistance, aimed at similar environments. Both types are essential in ensuring safety and reliability in electrical installations across various industries.</p></p>
