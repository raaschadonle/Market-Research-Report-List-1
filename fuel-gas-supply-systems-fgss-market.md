<p><p>Fuel Gas Supply Systems (FGSS) are vital in delivering gaseous fuels efficiently for various applications in industrial and commercial settings. The market can be segmented into low-pressure and high-pressure systems. Low-pressure systems operate at reduced pressure, making them suitable for applications like heating and cooking. In contrast, high-pressure systems are designed for applications that require a more forceful gas supply, such as power generation and heavy industry. Both types cater to specific operational needs, emphasizing safety and reliability in gas distribution.</p></p>
