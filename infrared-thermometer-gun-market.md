<p><p>The infrared thermometer gun market features two primary types based on targeting capabilities: single laser targeting and dual laser targeting. Single laser targeting utilizes one laser to pinpoint the measurement area, providing basic accuracy for general temperature readings. In contrast, dual laser targeting employs two lasers, creating a visual reference for a more precise measurement of the targeted spot, enhancing accuracy, especially in complex environments. Both types cater to different consumer needs, from basic to professional applications.</p></p>
