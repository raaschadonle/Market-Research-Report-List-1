<p><p>The intravenous fluid drip market is segmented into two primary types: macro drip and micro drip. Macro drip sets deliver larger drops per milliliter, typically suited for rapid fluid administration in emergencies or during surgeries, where speed is crucial. In contrast, micro drip sets provide smaller drops per milliliter, allowing for precise control over fluid delivery, making them ideal for pediatric patients or situations requiring careful monitoring. Each type serves specific clinical needs while ensuring patient safety and effective treatment.</p></p>
