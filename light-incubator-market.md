<p><p>The Light Incubator market is categorized into two main types: Cold Light and Heat Light. Cold Light incubators utilize fluorescent or LED lighting to provide a stable environment for sensitive biological cultures without raising temperatures. In contrast, Heat Light incubators employ incandescent or specialized heatlamps to create a controlled warm environment, essential for certain applications like tissue culture or microbial growth. Both types aim to provide optimal conditions for the growth and development of various biological specimens while maintaining specific temperature and light requirements.</p></p>
