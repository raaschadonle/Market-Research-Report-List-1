<p><strong>The Marine Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fire Tube Boiler</li><li>Water Tube Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine boilers market is primarily segmented into fire tube and water tube boilers. Fire tube boilers have a design where hot gases pass through tubes surrounded by water, making them compact and efficient for ships with low steam demands. In contrast, water tube boilers utilize water-filled tubes heated by external flames, allowing higher pressures and steam output, which is suitable for larger vessels. Both types are essential for marine applications, ensuring optimal performance and safety at sea.</p></p>
