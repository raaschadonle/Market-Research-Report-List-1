<p><strong>The Military Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV)</li><li>Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Military Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUV) market consists of two primary types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered to a surface vessel and operated by a human, ideal for real-time surveillance and operations in hostile environments. In contrast, AUVs operate independently, utilizing pre-programmed missions, making them suitable for tasks like underwater mapping and reconnaissance. Both types enhance military capabilities in intelligence gathering, mine detection, and underwater inspections, playing a crucial role in maritime operations.</p></p>
