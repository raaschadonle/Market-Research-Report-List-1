<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Instruments Market includes various types of equipment designed to evaluate material integrity without causing damage. Ultrasonic Test Equipment uses sound waves to detect flaws; Magnetic Particle Test Equipment detects surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials; Visual Inspection Equipment enables direct observation for flaws; Radiography Test Equipment employs X-rays or gamma rays for internal inspections; and Penetrant Test Equipment identifies surface-breaking defects using dyes. Other methods may include eddy current and thermography testing.</p></p>
