<p><p>The phytopathological disease diagnostic kit market includes serological and molecular tests. Serological tests detect plant pathogens by identifying specific antibodies or antigens in plant tissues, offering a relatively quick and cost-effective method for diagnostics. Conversely, molecular tests analyze the genetic material of pathogens using techniques like PCR, providing high specificity and sensitivity. Both testing types cater to agricultural needs, enabling timely disease management and improving crop yields by accurately diagnosing plant health issues.</p></p>
