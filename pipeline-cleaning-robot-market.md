<p><ul><li>Pressure-based Cleaning Robot</li><li>Tool-based Cleaning Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pipeline Cleaning Robot market comprises two main types: pressure-based cleaning robots and tool-based cleaning robots. Pressure-based cleaning robots utilize high-pressure water jets to dislodge debris, grease, and other blockages within pipelines, ensuring efficient cleaning and maintenance. In contrast, tool-based cleaning robots employ various mechanical tools and attachments to scrape, cut, or remove build-up from pipe surfaces. Both types enhance pipeline performance, reduce maintenance costs, and improve operational efficiency in sectors like water treatment, oil and gas, and sewage management.</p></p>
