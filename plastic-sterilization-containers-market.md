<p><p>The plastic sterilization containers market is categorized into two primary types: filter type and valve type. Filter type containers feature built-in filters that allow for the passage of steam and air while preventing contamination, ensuring effective sterilization. Valve type containers, on the other hand, utilize valves to regulate airflow and maintain optimal pressure during the sterilization process. Both types are designed to enhance the safety and efficiency of sterilization in medical and surgical settings, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
