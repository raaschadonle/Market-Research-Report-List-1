<p><p>The polystyrene tubes market features two main types: round bottom and conical bottom tubes. Round bottom tubes are commonly used for general laboratory applications, offering stability and uniform heat distribution. In contrast, conical bottom tubes are designed for efficient sedimentation and are ideal for applications requiring centrifugation. Both types provide excellent chemical resistance and clarity, making them suitable for various scientific and medical uses. The choice between the two depends on the specific requirements of the experiment or application.</p></p>
