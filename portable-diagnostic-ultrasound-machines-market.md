<p><strong>The Portable Diagnostic Ultrasound Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-mode</li><li>B-mode</li><li>C-mode</li><li>F-mode</li><li>M-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable diagnostic ultrasound machines encompass various modes defined by their imaging capabilities. A-mode (Amplitude mode) displays echoes as spikes, primarily used in ophthalmology. B-mode (Brightness mode) provides 2D grayscale images, facilitating organ visualization. C-mode (Contact mode) captures cross-sectional images, often for research. F-mode focuses on flow, aiding in blood vessel assessments. M-mode (Motion mode) tracks moving structures in real-time, commonly used in cardiology. Together, these modes enhance diagnostic accuracy across diverse medical applications.</p></p>
