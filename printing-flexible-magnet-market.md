PVC is popular for its durability and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications. PP is lightweight and offers good chemical resistance, ideal for less demanding environments. PET is known for its strength and clarity, often used for high-quality printing. Each material serves different needs in terms of performance and application.</p></p>
