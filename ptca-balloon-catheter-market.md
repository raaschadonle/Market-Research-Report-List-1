<p><p>The PTCA balloon catheter market is categorized into three types: compliant, semi-compliant, and non-compliant balloons. Compliant balloons expand more readily with lower pressures, allowing for gentle dilation of narrowed arteries. Semi-compliant balloons offer moderate compliance, providing a balance between expansion and support. Non-compliant balloons maintain their shape under higher pressures, making them ideal for precise, targeted dilation of stubborn lesions. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, influencing procedural outcomes in angioplasty treatments.</p></p>
