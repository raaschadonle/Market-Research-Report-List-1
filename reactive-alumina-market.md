<p><p>The Reactive Alumina market comprises two primary types: powdered form and sphered form. Powdered form reactive alumina is characterized by fine particles, offering enhanced reactivity for applications such as catalysts and adsorbents. In contrast, sphered form reactive alumina features a granulated structure, which improves flowability and reduces dust generation. Both forms are utilized across various industries, including petrochemicals, ceramics, and water treatment, to enhance performance and efficiency in processes such as filtration and chemical reactions.</p></p>
