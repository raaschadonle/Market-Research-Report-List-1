<p><p>The recombinant protein market encompasses various technologies, including molecular cloning and polymerase chain reaction (PCR). Molecular cloning involves isolating and replicating specific DNA segments to produce desired proteins, which are essential for research and therapeutic applications. PCR, on the other hand, is a technique used to amplify DNA sequences rapidly, facilitating the generation of sufficient quantities for further analysis. Both technologies are vital in biopharmaceutical development, diagnostics, and research, driving innovations in genetic engineering and protein production.</p></p>
