<p><p>The recuperative oxidizers market is primarily divided into two types: thermal and catalytic. Thermal oxidizers operate by using high temperatures to oxidize volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other pollutants, releasing heat that can be recaptured for energy efficiency. Catalytic oxidizers, on the other hand, utilize catalysts to facilitate the oxidation process at lower temperatures, resulting in reduced energy consumption and emissions. Both technologies serve essential roles in air pollution control, catering to diverse industrial applications and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
