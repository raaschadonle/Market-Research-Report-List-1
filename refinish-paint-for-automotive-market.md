<p><p>Refinish paint for the automotive market includes several types: solvent-borne, water-borne, and others. Water-borne paints, on the other hand, use water as the primary solvent, resulting in lower VOC emissions and a more environmentally friendly profile. "Others" encompasses niche products, such as powder coatings and specialty finishes, providing unique properties or applications tailored to specific automotive refinish needs. Each type serves diverse customer preferences and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
