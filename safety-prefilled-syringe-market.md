<p><p>The safety prefilled syringe market is categorized primarily into glass-based and plastic-based types. In contrast, plastic-based syringes are lightweight, unbreakable, and often more cost-effective, catering to high-volume use. The selection between glass and plastic depends on factors such as drug compatibility, storage requirements, and patient safety considerations.</p></p>
