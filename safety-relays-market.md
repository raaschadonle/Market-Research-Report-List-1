<p><ul><li>Electromechanical Relay</li><li>Solid State Relay</li><li>Thermal Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety relays market comprises three main types: electromechanical relays, solid-state relays, and thermal relays. Electromechanical relays use electromagnetic coils to open or close circuits, offering reliable physical isolation. Solid-state relays utilize semiconductor components for faster switching and higher durability, with no moving parts. Thermal relays monitor temperature changes to protect equipment from overheating. Each type serves distinct applications in automation and safety systems, ensuring effective control and protection in industrial environments.</p></p>
