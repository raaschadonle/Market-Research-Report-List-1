<p><p>The Sipping Tequilas market is primarily divided into two categories: Pure Tequila and Mixto Tequila. Pure Tequila, or 100% agave tequila, is made solely from blue agave and is known for its rich flavors and complexity, appealing to connoisseurs. In contrast, Mixto Tequila contains at least 51% agave, with the remainder being sugar cane or other spirits, making it more affordable but often less nuanced. These distinctions cater to diverse consumer preferences and budgets within the market.</p></p>
