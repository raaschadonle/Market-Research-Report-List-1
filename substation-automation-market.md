<p><p>The Substation Automation market is divided into two main types: transmission and distribution substations. Transmission substations are crucial for high-voltage power transmission over long distances, ensuring minimal energy loss and maintaining grid stability. They focus on monitoring and controlling bulk power flow. In contrast, distribution substations operate at lower voltage levels to deliver electricity to end-users. They prioritize local energy management, integrating renewable sources, and enhancing grid reliability. Both types leverage advanced automation technologies for improved efficiency and operational safety.</p></p>
