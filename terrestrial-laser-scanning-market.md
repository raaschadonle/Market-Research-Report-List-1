<p><p>Terrestrial Laser Scanning (TLS) is an advanced surveying technique that utilizes laser technology to create precise three-dimensional models of objects and environments. Additionally, advancements in scanning technology, such as smaller, more portable scanners and increased processing power, are making TLS more accessible to a wider range of users. The growing emphasis on infrastructure development and urban planning is also fueling market expansion, with organizations seeking efficient and reliable surveying methods to facilitate complex projects.
