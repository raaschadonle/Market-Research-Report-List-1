<p><p>The thermoelectric cooling system market is primarily segmented into two types: single-stage modules and multistage modules. Single-stage modules are efficient for modest temperature differences, providing compact cooling solutions suitable for small devices and applications. In contrast, multistage modules offer enhanced cooling capabilities, enabling refrigeration of larger volumes or achieving lower temperatures by stacking multiple cascaded stages. This bifurcation allows diverse applications, from consumer electronics to industrial processes, catering to varying cooling requirements across different sectors.</p></p>
