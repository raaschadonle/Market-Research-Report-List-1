<p><ul><li>Cooled Infrared Detectors</li><li>Uncooled Infrared Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermographic camera market is primarily divided into two types: cooled infrared detectors and uncooled infrared detectors. Cooled infrared detectors operate at low temperatures, enhancing sensitivity and enabling the detection of finer temperature differences, making them ideal for applications like military and aerospace. Uncooled infrared detectors, on the other hand, function at ambient temperatures, offering lower costs and ease of use, making them popular for commercial and residential applications, such as building inspections and firefighting.</p></p>
