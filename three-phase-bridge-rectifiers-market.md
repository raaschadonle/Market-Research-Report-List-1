<p><p>The three-phase bridge rectifier market consists of three main types: SMD/SMT, Screw Mount, and Through Hole. SMD/SMT rectifiers are designed for surface mounting, offering compactness and automation in assembly. Screw Mount rectifiers provide ease of installation with secure connections, ideal for high-power applications. Through Hole rectifiers feature leads that pass through a circuit board, ensuring durability and strong mechanical support, making them suitable for robust, heavy-duty environments. Each type caters to specific application needs in electronic designs.</p></p>
