<p><ul><li>WiFi</li><li>DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video door phone market is primarily divided into two types: WiFi and DECT. WiFi video door phones connect to the home network, allowing users to monitor their doorbell through smartphones and other devices via internet connectivity. This enables real-time video streaming and remote access. On the other hand, DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) video door phones utilize a dedicated frequency for secure, cordless communication, offering reliable connectivity without relying on internet stability, making them ideal for outdoor use.</p></p>
